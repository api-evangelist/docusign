The Docusign MCP Server standardizes how specialized legal AI platforms connect to Docusign's Intelligent Agreement Management (IAM) system to query agreement data and trigger workflows. It outlines two integration patterns: one where IAM invokes specialized partner tools, and one where partner platforms invoke Docusign agreement data and workflows.
